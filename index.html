How I Work

Process & approach.

Discover

Research, user interviews, competitive review, and behavioral mapping to understand the real problem before designing a solution.

Define

Synthesizing findings into clear problem frames, user journeys, and design principles that keep the team aligned on what matters.

Design

High-fidelity, polished work from day one — flows, components, interactions, and a visual language that earns trust.

Deliver

Close collaboration with engineering to ship with quality, followed by measurement and iteration to keep improving.

I've spent my career helping people navigate complex systems — financial tools, AI products, behavioral platforms — by designing experiences that feel clear, calm, and motivating.

At Financial Finesse, I led product design for AI-driven and platform experiences that reach employees through employer-sponsored wellness programs. That work required equal parts strategic thinking, behavioral psychology, and craft — balancing business goals with deeply human needs.

I care about the details that most people never notice — the micro-interaction that reduces anxiety, the copy that actually answers the question, the flow that makes someone feel competent instead of confused. That's where I spend a lot of my energy.

Momentum matters
